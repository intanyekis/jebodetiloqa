Jamal Musiala channels Lionel Messi to beat six players and score wonderful solo goal in Bayern Muni

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Jamal Musiala channeled his inner Lionel Messi to score a sensational goal for Bayern Munich against Wolfsburg in the Bundesliga.

The German midfielder is having an excellent season and added to it with his goal against Wolfsburg, which was Bayern’s fourth as they secured a 4-2 win away from home.

The win was a crucial one as it put the Bavarians back in control of the title race having not won any of their three league games in 2023 thus far.

Musiala’s goal started off on the right side of the final third as he found his way inwards, and he beat three defenders on the way, slicing through the opposition defence before slotting the ball into the net on the near post.

The goal was Musiala’s 10th of the Bundesliga season and in addition to his six assists, he has the most goal contributions out of all the players in the league after 19 games played.

After Robert Lewandowski’s departure, it is Musiala who has taken up the responsibility of leading the Bayern Munich attack and the 19-year-old has impressed massively over the course of this season thus far.

It’s not just for Bayern that he is doing well – for Germany, he was their star performer despite their group stage exit and he earned plaudits for his maturity with the team.

Bayern Munich have had a stuttering season so far despite being on top of the league as they have shown flashes of inconsistency, being just a point clear at the top with Union Berlin, Borussia Dortmund and RB Leipzig behind them.

Musiala, though, is taking matters into his own hands and has been one of the league’s best players, showing quality beyond his years and will undoubtedly be a key figure for club and country for years to come.
