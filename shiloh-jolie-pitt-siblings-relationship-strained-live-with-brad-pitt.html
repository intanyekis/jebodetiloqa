Angelina Jolie and Brad Pitt's messy 2016 divorce and longstanding feud exposed several shocking revelations about their family, particularly the relationship dynamics with their six children. This article will explore Shiloh's relationship with her siblings and Brad's relationship with all his children.

Inside Shiloh Jolie-Pitt's Relationship With Her Siblings Amid Brad And Angelina's Divorce

During their marriage, Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie dutifully raised their six children to be close-knit and to embrace family values. Even after the pair's tumultuous divorce, Jolie ensured her children did not deviate from these values. However, the Jolie-Pitt kids appeared to take sides in their parents' feud over time. While Maddox and Pax's relations with Brad become strained, Shiloh's bond with him got stronger. She made headlines in March 2024 regarding her intentions to move out of Angelina's home once she turned 18. Shiloh had plans to live with Brad. In light of this, it appeared as though the siblings picked sides in their parents' feud, but what is the state of their relations as brothers and sisters?

According to The List, there were speculations that Shiloh Jolie-Pitt and her older siblings, Maddox, Pax, and Zahara, were at odds regarding the situation between their parents. It was reported that Shiloh was in a dilemma due to her older brothers' estrangement from their father, Brad Pitt. It is safe to say that even megastars like Taylor Swift know of the kind of pain all too well. Amid Brad and Angelina Jolie's custody battle, Maddox testified against the Troy actor. The young adult cited domestic abuse issues, claiming that Brad got physical during an altercation.

Pax was also quite vocal on Instagram when he shared a scathing Father's Day post in 2023 and hurled expletives at Brad Pitt. Before the shocking development, Brad was reportedly wary that Angelina Jolie would turn the kids against him. Close sources relayed that amid the high tension in the Jolie-Pitt family, Shiloh struggled to navigate her relationship with her brothers and their father. An insider for Life & Style Magazine revealed that Shiloh tried to broker peace in her family while understanding the grievances. The source stated:

"She saw both points of view without judging either harshly. Maddox has clearly sided with Angelina, but Shiloh was desperate for her family to be whole and happy again."
