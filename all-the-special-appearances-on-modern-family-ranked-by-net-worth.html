Modern Family, a beloved American sitcom, aired from 2009 to 2020, spanning 11 seasons. The show, created by Christopher Lloyd and Steven Levitan, followed the lives of Jay Pritchett and his family, which included his second wife, his stepson, and his two adult children and their families. One of the many reasons for the show's success was its stellar cast, which featured the leading ensemble and many special guest appearances.

Greg Kinnear - $20 Million

According to IMDB, Greg Kinnear, an accomplished actor with a net worth of $20 million, made a memorable guest appearance in Modern Family's Season 3, Episode 14. Kinnear has an impressive acting resume, including standout roles in films such as As Good as It Gets and Little Miss Sunshine. In his Modern Family episode, he portrayed Tad, Phil's new business partner, adding a humorous and slightly awkward dynamic to the show.

Nathan Fillion - $20 Million

In four episodes of Modern Family Season 9, Nathan Fillion appears as Rainer Shine, a local weatherman who becomes romantically involved with Haley. Fillion's charm and wit made his character a memorable addition to the show. Fillion is known for his roles in the television series Firefly, Castle, and The Rookie and his voice work in the animated film Monsters University. According to Money INC, Nathan earned a whopping $100,000 per episode during his role as Richard Castle on the ABC series Castle and currently has an impressive $20 Million net worth.

Nathan Lane - $25 Million

According to Screen Rant, Nathan Lane played the flamboyant and extravagant Pepper Saltzman, a close friend of Mitchell and Cameron in Modern Family. Lane's portrayal of Pepper was a fan favorite, and he appeared in several episodes throughout the series. Lane is a celebrated actor and singer, known for his roles in The Producers, The Birdcage and for voicing Timon in The Lion King. He has acquired a stunning $25 Million net worth owing to his long and illustrious career.

Matt Dillon - $40 Million

Matt Dillon guest-starred in Modern Family's Season 2, Episode 13. Dillon has an extensive acting career, with notable works like There's Something About Mary and Crash under his belt. In his Modern Family appearance, he played Robbie Sullivan, Claire's ex-boyfriend, showcasing his talent for portraying a sleazy yet entertaining character. Over his long acting career, Dillon has accumulated a stunning $40 Million net worth.

Jordan Peele - $50 Million

Jordan Peele is an actor, comedian, writer, and director. He is best known for his work on the sketch comedy series Key & Peele, which he co-created and starred alongside Keegan-Michael Key. Peele made a guest appearance in Modern Family's Season 5, Episode 7, titled A Fair to Remember, where he played the character of Derrick. In 2017, he co-wrote and directed Get Out, which received widespread critical acclaim and earned over $255 Million worldwide. Attributing to his distinctive multi-talent, Peele has earned himself a stunning net worth of $50 Million.

Elizabeth Banks - $50 Million

Elizabeth Banks, boasting a net worth of $50 million, had a recurring role in Modern Family as Sal, the wild friend of Mitchell and Cameron. Banks appeared in multiple episodes across various seasons, bringing her unique energy to the show. She is also well-known for her roles in popular films such as The Hunger Games and Pitch Perfect.

Leslie Mann - $150 Million

Leslie Mann appears in Season 5, Episode 18 of Modern Family as Katie, a woman who mistakenly believes that Jay is her blind date, as per Collider. Mann is an established actress known for performing in movies like George of the Jungle, Knocked Up, Rio, and This Is 40. With a career spanning over three decades, she has accumulated a massive net worth of $150 Million combined with her husband, Judd Apatow.

Matthew Broderick - $200 Million

Matthew Broderick, a renowned actor with a massive net worth of $200 Million, appeared in a guest role in Season 4 Episode 8 of Modern Family. He appears as Dave, a man who mistakenly believes he is on a date with Cameron. Broderick's comedic timing and awkward interactions with Cameron made for a hilarious episode. Broderick is best known for his roles in Godzilla, Ferris Bueller's Day Off, The Producers, and The Lion King, where he voiced Simba.

Edward Norton - $300 Million

Edward Norton is an acclaimed actor, director, and producer with notable roles in films such as Fight Club, American History X, and Birdman. Norton, known for his versatile acting skills, appeared in Season 1, Episode 9 of Modern Family as Izzy LaFontaine, a washed-up rock musician hired by Claire as a surprise for her husband's birthday. Norton's performance added a unique flavor to the episode. He has been nominated for three Academy Awards and is one of the highest-paid actors in the world. Norton currently boasts a staggering net worth of $300 Million, as per Wealthy Gorilla.

Kevin Hart - $450 Million

As per The Strive, Kevin Hart is the wealthiest actor on this list, with a staggering net worth of $450 Million. He played the role of Andre, Phil's neighbor and friend in Season 2, Episode 12 of Modern Family. Hart's comedic timing and chemistry with the cast made his appearance memorable and enjoyable. Hart is a successful comedian, actor, and producer known for his stand-up comedy specials and roles in films like Jumanji: Welcome to the Jungle, Ride Along, and Central Intelligence. Kevin made a whopping $90 million from touring, movies, merchandise, endorsements, and other sources from August 2015 to August 2016.

Modern Family's success can be attributed to its talented main cast and the numerous special guest appearances that added depth and variety to the show. These guest stars brought their unique talents and charm to the series, creating memorable moments and contributing to its enduring popularity. Their impressive careers and accomplishments outside Modern Family demonstrate the caliber of talent that graced the screen during the 11-season run.
