John Lydon

Birthname John Joseph Lydon Birthdate January 31, 1956 Birthplace Holloway, London, United Kingdom Profession Singer Net worth $15,000,000 Source of Wealth Music, TV Nationality United Kingdom Country United Kingdom Marital Status Married (Nora Forster)

John Lydon is a British television presenter and singer-songwriter. From 1975 through 1978, he served as the lead singer of Sex Pistols, a punk rock band. In addition to being the founder, Lydon is also the lead singer of Public Image Ltd (PiL).

Although Public Image Ltd (PiL) was not as commercially successful as Sex Pistols, it has released a total of eight albums and a number of singles, including "Rise", "Death Disco", and "Public Image," before the band went into hiatus in 1993. The band re-formed in 2009. In addition to being a musician, Lydon is also a presenter of many TV shows in Belgium, the United Kingdom, and the United States.

Also known as Johnny Rotten, John Joseph Lydon was born on  January 31, 1956 in Holloway, London, England. He is married to Germany-based publishing heiress Nora Forster.
