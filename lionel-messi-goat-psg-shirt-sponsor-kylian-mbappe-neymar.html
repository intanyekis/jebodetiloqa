Lionel Messi will officially have 'GOAT' on his Paris Saint-Germain shirt next season thanks to new

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Thursday, June 20, 2024

Lionel Messi will officially have the 'GOAT' acronym on his Paris Saint-Germain shirt next season, with many believing this confirms his status as the greatest of all time.

But, of course, it's not just Messi - the whole PSG team will have the abbreviation on their kits thanks to a new sponsorship deal.

GOAT, one of the leading marketplaces for trainers, clothing and accessories, signed a deal to sponsor the PSG shirt for the upcoming season.

They will have their brand name on the sleeve of the French champions' new shirts - and there was only one player who was going to model it.

Messi, who is entering his second season at PSG after sensationally leaving boyhood club Barcelona last year, proudly donned the new jersey, showing off the 'GOAT' logo.

The Parisians will be hoping the kit, which features a large white vertical stripe with red trim down the middle, is the one that they finally win the Champions League in.

Kylian Mbappe, Marquinhos, Presnel Kimpembe, Achraf Hakimi, and Marco Verratti were also present for the club's 2022/23 official home kit release,

Women's stars Sakina Karchaoui and Grace Geyoro joined the men's squad for the promotional material.

Neymar was also seen in the new kit, which could mean that he will still be in the French capital next season, despite talk that he could leave.

There have been rumours that each one of their world-class trio could depart (Mbappe, in particular, before he signed a new deal with the club), with both Neymar and Messi linked with a return to Barcelona.

But if donning the new kit means you're staying at the club, then PSG will still have all three superstars for the upcoming season.
