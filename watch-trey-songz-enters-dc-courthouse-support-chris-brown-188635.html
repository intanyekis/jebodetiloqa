Trey Songz Enters DC Courthouse To Support Chris Brown

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, September 2, 2024

Chris Brown‘s legal woes continued to escalate after the singer was arrested after an altercation reportedly broke out in Washington D.C. this past weekend. The singer and his bodyguard were allegedly arrested on assault Sunday morning.

As previously reported, Brown was allegedly approached by two women in front of the W Hotel at 4:25AM as he was heading back after making a scheduled appearance at The Park at Fourteenth for Howard University’s annual Homecoming festival. As Breezy posed for the flick, two men allegedly tried to photobomb the flick, causing the singer to elicit a gay slur.

“I’m not into this gay s**t, I’m into boxing,” Brown said according to police, and threw a punch at one of the men. The victim was reportedly taken to a nearby hospital, suffering a broken nose. He is said to be pressing charges against Brown.

Brown is expected to be in court this afternoon. His mother and singer Trey Songz were spotted entering the D.C. courthouse to support him during his arraignment. See footage of Songz’ entrance below.
