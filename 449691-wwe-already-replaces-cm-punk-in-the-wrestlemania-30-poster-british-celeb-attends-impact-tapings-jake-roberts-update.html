WWE Already Replaces CM Punk in WrestleMania 30 Poster

In what looks to be an even further sign that WWE might not have any plans to try and bring CM Punk back to the company in time for WrestleMania 30, the company has replaced him on the official WrestleMania promo poster with the 'Mania logo.

The poster now features John Cena, Mark Henry, Brock Lesnar, The Undertaker, Alberto Del Rio and Randy Orton, and Punk has been cropped out.

We reported earlier this evening that WWE has announced Ric Flair as Punk's replacement for the WrestleMania AXXESS events.

British Celeb Attends Impact Tapings

WZ reader Adam Cailler has sent in word that British TV personality Mark "The Beast" Labbett, was in attendance for today's Impact Wrestling tapings in Manchester. Labbett is best known for his role as a "chaser" on the ITV game show "The Chase."

Roberts Returning to the Ring

Hall of Fame inductee Jake "The Snake" Roberts will be making two in-ring wrestling appearances in New Jersey and Illinois in the coming weeks. Roberts will be wrestling at Jersey Championship Wrestling's events on 2/21 and 2/22 in Union, NJ.

Roberts will also be facing Duece Trey at Chicago Style Wrestling's event at Guerin Prep High School in River Grove, IL on Saturday, February 8th.
