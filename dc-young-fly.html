nye na-b DC Young Fly? Wikipedia, Net uru, Egwu na Youtube

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Sunday, August 25, 2024

DC Young Fly Wiki

DC Young Fly bụ onyinye African American kacha amasị amụrụ John Whitfield, onye na-eme ihe nkiri. O bilitere na kpakpando site na omume ya dị ka onye na-eme ihe nkiri, onye na-agụ egwú, onye ọbịa, onye na-agụ egwú na onye na-agụ ọchị na nyiwe mgbasa ozi dị ka Instagram, YouTube, na Vines. A mụrụ onye America na May 2, 1992 na Atlanta, Georgia. Dc Young Fly na-arụsikwa ọrụ ike na nyiwe mgbasa ozi ọha dịka Twitter, Instagram, Facebook na YouTube.

John tolitere n'obodo Adamsville, n'akụkụ ọdịda anyanwụ nke agbata obi ha. Ọtụtụ mgbe, ya na ụmụnne ya abụọ na-anọkọ n'okporo ụzọ. Mpaghara ya so n'ime ihe kachasị dị ize ndụ n'ihi na omume mpụ bụ ihe a na-ahụkarị, na ịzụ ahịa ọgwụ ọjọọ na-eme ọbụna ụmụntakịrị, nke ọtụtụ n'ime ha mara site na ndị uwe ojii.

Whitfield malitere rapping 2011 mgbe e gbuchara nwanne ya nwoke; abụ ya bụ ụzọ isi sọpụrụ ndụ ya cheta ya mgbe ọ na-anọghị ya. Dịka onye na-anụ ọkụ n'obi maka ihe nkiri DC, ọ chọtara aha maka onwe ya, DC Young Fly. Mgbe ihe omume ahụ na-egbu mgbu gasịrị, o kpebiri ịtụgharị ndụ ya, hapụ ndụ ndị otu, ma lekwasị anya na ndụ ya na egwu ya. N'agbanyeghị na ezinụlọ ya abụghị ọha ọha, nna ya bụ pastọ, na ọ nwụrụ na mgbede nke Christmas na 2013.

Ọrụ DC Young Fly Rap

Ọ wepụtara "Right now" na "Ọ dịghị igbo" na 2016; nke ahụ n'ime oge a, ọ pụtara n'ọtụtụ akụkụ nke ihe nkiri ihe nkiri MTV a; Anụ apụọla.

Ọrụ YouTube

ya YouTube chanel nwere ihe karịrị nde 3 nde ndị debanyere aha yana ihe karịrị nde 19 na mkpokọta. Ọnụọgụ ndị a esighị n'ọdụ ụgbọ elu DC Young Fly nke ya kama ọ bụ ọdụ ụgbọ elu niile o nwere. Otu n'ime vidiyo YouTube ya ama ama bụ mgbe ọ na-ata Michael Blackson nke nwere ihe karịrị nde nlele n'otu izu.

Ndụ nkeonwe

Ọzọkwa, o nwere nwa nwanyị na Jacky Oh, onye bụ nsụgharị. A mụrụ nwa ha nwanyị na October 2016, na aha ya bụ Nova Whitfield. Ọ bụ ezie na ọ lụbeghị Jacky, ha abụọ adịla nso ugbu a n'ihi na ewepụrụ Nova.

DC Young Fly Net bara uru

Ugbu a, ọnụ ahịa DC Young Fly bụ naanị $ 5 nde. Ụfọdụ ego sitere na ọwa YouTube ya, "Dc Young Fly," nke na-eme ya ihe ruru $50,000 kwa afọ.
