Pregnant Stassi Schroeder Shows Off Growing Baby Bump In Tiny Top: 37 Weeks &amp; Over It

Posted by Reinaldo Massengill on Monday, June 24, 2024

Stassi Schroeder is reaching the end of her first pregnancy and she's admittedly 'over it.' In a new photo featuring her bare baby bump, the Bravo alum revealed she's 37 weeks pregnant!

Stassi Schroeder is ready to meet her baby girl! The Bravo alum, 32, who’s expecting her first child with husband Beau Clark, shared a (relatable) pregnancy update on December 15. — “37 Weeks & Over It,” she wrote over a mirror selfie of her growing belly, via Instagram Stories. Stassi posed in a white lace bra and a pair of grey sweatpants.

The expectant mother has spent her third trimester trolling herself over “feeling huge” on social media. (Although, she looks absolutely stunning!) In early October, Stassi shared a photo of herself eating a chicken wing with the caption, “Sex symbol right here.” Meanwhile, her growing bump was captured poking out of her matching sweatshirt and pants set.

The comedy continued when Stassi and Beau dressed as a pregnant zombie couple for Halloween. “Framing this for her nursery,” she wrote alongside a photo of the pair that showed a fake zombie baby attached to her pregnant belly. The costume choice didn’t come as a surprise to Stassi’s fans, who are well aware of her love for cemeteries and the spookier things in life.

Let’s not forget when Stassi and Beau got engaged in a cemetery in July 2019. The unique proposal famously aired at the end of Vanderpump Rules‘ eighth season — which marked Stassi’s last season before she was let go from the show in June.

While 2020 hasn’t been ideal for most (with the COVID-19 pandemic and the continued fight against unrest in America) Stassi is counting her blessings. On November 26, she shared a professional photo, via Instagram, featuring herself and Beau cradling her baby bump. “This obvs wasn’t my favorite year, but this is the first time I truly know the meaning of thankful,” Stassi captioned her post.

Stassi and Beau, whose “dream wedding” in Italy was postponed due to the coronavirus pandemic, tied the knot in September. At the time, Stassi shared on Instagram that the couple decided to get married on the date they had initially planned. “Married Sept. 2020,” she wrote alongside a video from the intimate backyard ceremony. Stassi added that the couple will hopefully” have their “Italian dream wedding in Oct. 2021.”
