Lance Reddick's Wife Stephanie Reddick Keeps Her Life Lowkey &amp; Is His Best Supporter

Posted by Martina Birk on Saturday, August 31, 2024

Lance Reddick's wife was the brains behind their low-key wedding in 2011. She prefers to keep away from the spotlight despite occasionally appearing to support her movie star husband at Hollywood events.

Lance Reddick, who plays Charon in "John Wick," became a married man for the second time in 2011 when he wed Stephanie Reddick, whom he met more than a decade before they exchanged vows. The actor considers his relationship with Stephanie as a union that would have been illegal when he was a child.

Stephanie is a private individual who does not have a strong media presence. She is pro-human and supports groups of society living with challenges, such as the LGBTQ+ community. She does not hesitate to attend significant events to clap for her famous husband in person.

Stephanie Is an Activist & Helps Raise Awareness in Saving Lives of the LGBTQ Community

Stephanie lives a very private life, without many publicly shared opinions on tense societal topics. Still, she does try to support the disadvantaged groups in society. Her Instagram account is private, yet the only information available to users without access to her profile is an LGBTQ Suicide Prevention Lifeline link on her bio.

The link opens up to the 988 Suicide & Crisis Lifeline website, giving those struggling with mental health a direct source of help. The website advises the LGBTQ+ community to acknowledge that they are not alone, talk to someone about their thoughts, build a support network, and devise a safety plan.

The website also announced that '988' has been the designated dialing code for the Suicide and Crisis Lifeline since July 16, 2022. In keeping with her support for the voiceless, Stephanie contributed to the #MeToo movement on Twitter. She wrote about how she was also violated by previously accused Robert Knepper in 1989.
