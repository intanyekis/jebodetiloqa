The Viral Claims About Taylor Swift's Parents' House Were Likely False

During the 2020s, several viral videos claimed that Taylor Swift owned an abandoned mansion. Furthermore, many of Swift's fans believed that the abandoned mansion was bought by the pop star for her parents to live in. As it turns out, every aspect of those videos almost certainly were completely false.

After the videos with those claims went viral, one of Swift's fans posted a link to Realtor.com. According to that link, it appears that the mansion seen in the videos is located in Lowhill Township, Pennsylvania.

Since Swift is so beloved, the media reports on everything they can find out about her. For example, the media has reported on the homes that Swift has bought for her family in the past.

None of those reports indicate that Swift bought a home in Pennsylvania. With that in mind, it is very unlikely the pop star has ever had anything to do with the mansion seen in the viral videos.

In 2011, it was reported that Swift bought her parents a home for a staggering $1.4 million. That home was located in Nashville, Tennessee, was over 5,000 square feet in size, had three bedrooms, and had been renovated.

Another report from 2011 also suggested that Swift may have bought her parents a home in Nashville for $2.5 million.

Taylor Swift's Parents Almost Certainly Lead A Luxury Lifestyle

Following some concerning rumors that circulated about the living situation Taylor Swift's parents were in, some fans were worried. In reality, it appears to be almost completely certain that Swift's parents have a luxurious lifestyle.

As of the time of this writing, very little can be confirmed about how Andrea and Scott Swift live today. Given that Scott and Andrea Swift want to maintain their privacy as much as they can, it is unknown where Swift's parents are living today. With that in mind, it begs an obvious question. How is it known that Swift's parents almost certainly have a luxurious lifestyle?

According to Cosmopolitan, Andrea and Scott Swift got married on February 20, 1988 . Taylor Swift's parents reportedly tied the knot in Houston, Texas.

Some facts are known about Swift's parents and all of them indicate that Andrea and Scott could afford a very comfortable lifestyle. In fact, based on what is known about Swift's history, it seems clear that her parents would have a great lifestyle if their daughter never sold a single record.

In 2024, Cosmopolitan published an article focused on Swift's parents. As a part of that article, Cosmopolitan revealed how successful Scott and Andrea Swift were before their daughter became famous.

According to that report, Scott Swift worked as a stockbroker for Merrill Lynch and then was promoted to vice president. Similarly, Andrea Swift also had an impressive job as she worked as a marketing manager at an advertising agency.

As a 2024 article from People reveals, the couple's financial success played a role in their ability to help Swift launch her music career. Thanks to how accomplished Andrea and Scott Swift were, Swift's mom was able to leave work to go on the road to help launch her daughter's career.

Based on what is known about Andrea and Scott Swfit's history and careers, they would have had to make some huge mistakes to be struggling today.

One of the other key factors in how Taylor Swift's parents live today is how close they are to their daughter. From all accounts, Andrea and Scott Swift are both extremely close with their daughter. With that in mind, it seems inconceivable that Swift's parents wouldn't have a fantastic lifestyle.
