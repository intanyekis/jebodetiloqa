Rowan Blanchard Wiki Biography

Rowan Eleanor Blanchard was born on the 14th October 2001, in Los Angeles, California USA, and is an actress, perhaps best known to the world for her portrayal of Riley Matthews in the TV series “Girl Meets World”, and also as Jackie in the TV series “The Goldbergs”, among many other differing roles she has secured so far in her career.

Have you ever wondered how rich Rowan Blanchard is, as of early 2018? According to authoritative sources, it has been estimated that Blanchard’s net worth is as high as $3 million, an amount earned through her successful career, active since the mid- 2000s.

The oldest child born to Mark Blanchard-Boulbol and Elizabeth, who both are yoga instructors, Rowan is of mixed ancestry – her paternal grandfather moved to the US from the Middle East and is of Armenian ancestry, while her paternal grandmother has English, Swedish and Danish heritage.

Rowan was thrown into show business at the rather young age of five, but it wasn’t until she was nine in 2010 and the role of Caitlin in the TV series “Dance-A-Lot Robot”, that she landed any notable role. After her debut, Rowan appeared in the romantic comedy film “The Back-up Plan” the same year, next to Jennifer Lopez, Alex O’Loughlin and Michaela Watkins, while the following year she featured in the adventure comedy film “Spy Kids 4: All the Time in the World”, starring next to Jessica Alba and Joel McHale, which launched her to stardom and increased her net worth by a large margin, thanks to success of the film at the box office, grossing over $85 million.

In 2014 Rowan was chosen for her most prominent role so far, Riley Matthews in the TV comedy-drama series “Girl Meets World”, and appeared in all 72 episodes through 2017, which significantly increased her net worth. The role also earned her several award nominations, including the Young Artist Award in the category for the Outstanding Young Ensemble in a TV series. While in the cast of the popular series, Rowan’s name became sought by Hollywood producers and directors, and as a result, in 2015 she was given the role of Cleo in the comedy adventure television film “Invisible Sister”, starring with Paris Berelc and Karan Brar, which further increased her net worth. Also, Rowan starred in the adventure film “A World Away” with Nadine Velazquez and Mary Lynn Rajskub (2017), while most recently she began portraying Jackie Geary in the TV comedy series “The Goldbergs”, which has also added a considerable amount to her net worth.

She is now working on the action-adventure film “A Wrinkle in Time”, which is scheduled for release late in 2018.

When it comes to her personal life, Rowan identifies as ‘queer’, as she is open to dating with both boys and girls. She is a public activist, and has spoken at the UN Women and US National Committee’s annual conferences, about such topics as human rights, feminism and gun violence.
