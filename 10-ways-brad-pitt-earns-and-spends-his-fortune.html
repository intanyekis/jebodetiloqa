American actor and film producer Brad Pitt is one of the most well-known actors in Hollywood. Not only for his prowess but also because of carving out a position for himself in the cinema industry despite having a modest start. Brad Pitt has also received his first Oscar for acting at the Academy Awards. He had 186 nominations and 106 awards after starring in numerous high profile movies after he entered the entertainment scene in the late 1980s. Additionally, he has made millions of dollars. Brad Pitt has a reported net worth of $300 million, and his money is still expanding, according to the most recent revision of Forbes. His usual yearly earnings are comfortably in the millions.

Additionally, Pitt owns his own production company and is a skilled producer in addition to his acting career. He invests most of his wealth in real estate and charitable endeavors. Discover how Pitt earns and spends his wealth by continuing to read.

Earns: Brand Endorsements

Brad Pitt has been chosen by the Kering-owned brand to star in itsad campaigncelebrating the company's 75th anniversary.The prestigious menswear manufacturer Brioni in Rome is constantly expanding its list of well-known brand ambassadors. Pierce Brosnan, Anthony Hopkins, and Samuel L. Jackson are all Hollywood giants who were preceded in the role by Pitt. Pitt and Brioni have a history together because the American actor and producer frequently walk the red carpet wearing the company's clothing. At the Venice and Cannes Film Festivals, Pitt wore black custom tuxedos from Brioni.

Spends: Donations To Calamity Victims

Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie have always loved the environment and a desire to surround themselves with humanitarians. When they traveled to Haiti following the nation's devastation by a terrible earthquake in 2010, it was one of their most significant acts of humanitarianism. Jolie and Pitt contributed $1 million to Doctors without Borders, a Haitian emergency response organization, to aid in the provision of healthcare to the country's citizens.

Earns: Wine Production

A new wine that Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie invested in and marketed was unveiled in the spring of 2013; it was produced on the 150 acres of vineyard on the Chateau Miraval, which is located south of their French home. The wine is called Miraval Rose, and the ex-original couple's run of 6,000 bottles distributed in shops worldwide sold out in just five hours. Customers believed the hype, and reviewers couldn't wait to gush about it.

Spends: Buying A Superyacht

If the price tag for a massive boat is outrageous, Brad Pitt and his then-partner Jolie spent an additional $200,000 simply to adorn their superyacht, which they purchased for a staggering $322 million. It is assumed that celebrities will go to whatever lengths to ensure that they can navigate the oceans of the world with the luxurious and extravagant superyacht. We're too poor to comprehend. We know that sometimes it is preferable to travel across the sea than on a packed highway or a plane. Yachts are occasionally required.

Earns: Film Production

Together with his then-wife Jennifer Aniston and movie producer Brad Grey, Brad Pitt invested money in their own production business, Plan B Entertainment, in 2001. By 2004, Aniston and Pitt had divorced, Grey had left the industry to take the helm at Paramount Pictures, and Pitt had emerged as the only shareholder. Pitt has invested in several successful ventures while using the company to flex his producing skills. Movies The Departed, Moonlight, and 12 Years a Slave were three of the company's productions that won the Best Picture in The Oscars.

Spends: Acquiring A Massive Villa

Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie purchased an 18,000-square-foot palatial home for themselves in the Italian region of Valpolicella in 2010. The Italian villa has two swimming pools, a home theater, a vineyard, eight baths, and 15 bedrooms. The family decided to buy the property because they fell in love with Italy while Angelina Jolie was filming her movie, The Tourist. They spent $41 million on their acquisition.

Earns: Acting Career

Even today, Pitt earns millions of dollars from his film roles. His portrayal of a 1960s stuntman in the prominent Hollywood director Tarantino's film Once Upon a Time in... Hollywood garnered him his first acting Oscar. Still, due to different payment procedures, his upfront payout for the movie was much less than the $20 million. Pitt also earned from major blockbusters like World War Z. Pitt undoubtedly isn't having financial trouble because his payout structure differs from film to film. Pitt is a producer as well.

Spends: Foundation With Angelina Jolie

Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie launched the Jolie-Pitt Foundation in 2006. They established the organization to address and aid situations around the world and to advance their altruistic humanitarian endeavors. Tax documents show that 2006 was a busy first year for them, with $8 million stated to have been spent on various organizations by their foundation during the year. The two charities to which they gave the most money that year were Doctors Without Borders and Global Action for Children.

Earns: Hotel Business In Croatia

In 2016, Brad Pitt visited Sibenik, Croatia, for a luxurious and well deserved vacation. He stayed at the Dogusevom Hotel while he was there. He spent some time talking to the building's architect, and that same architect later revealed to a local newspaper that Pitt had ambitions to develop his own hotel in Croatia. One of the ten individuals that accompanied Pitt on a walking tour of the area in search of a location for the project was the architect Nikola Basic. According to reports, the project cost $2.5 billion.

Spends: Purchasing A Winery

Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie paid $35 million for the French estate Chateau Miraval in 2008. There are 35 bedrooms within the chateau, and the road alone is three miles wide. A moat, vineyards, trees, and perhaps a forest encircle it. It resembles a palace from a fantastic book. It also turned out to be the best location for Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie's decision to perform a private wedding ceremony there in 2014. This is because it has the standard fantasy story setting.
