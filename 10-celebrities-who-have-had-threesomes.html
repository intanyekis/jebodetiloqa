The elusive threesome: every man's dream, and apparently some women's as well. As soon as a young man hits puberty, he starts thinking about sex. Then pretty much right away after that, he thinks about having sex with two women at once. Because what could be better than having sex with one woman? That is easy. Having sex with two women of course. Well, I suppose three would be even better but now we are really getting out there in fantasy land. This post isn't about fantasies, it is about reality, and the reality is a lot of celebrities have had threesomes.

Whether they be male or female, celebrities are not that much different than you or I. They have the same thoughts and the same fantasies. Well, they might be a little different, because while a lot of us might dream of threesomes, the celebrities on this list have made it happen.

Some of these celebs admitted they have had threesomes, others have had people spill the beans without their approval. Still, others wrote about or even videotaped their threesomes. Some of them have even done the threesomes with their girlfriends or their wives. A threesome with your wife? What?

Here is TheRichest's list of 10 Celebrities Who Have Had A Threesome.

10. Charlie Sheen, Bree Olson, And Brooke Mueller

Just like Tiger Woods, Charlie Sheen just had to be on this list? Threesome? Charlie? I bet Charlie could be on this list all by himself along with numerous women he had threesomes with. Charlie calls his ladies Goddesses and has lived with two women at once, and slept in the same bed with both, so it is pretty safe to say threesomes are not a big deal to him. One threesome he had was with his ex-wife Brooke Mueller and adult film star Bree Olson. Bree revealed that she engaged in a threesome with Charlie and Brooke, at Charlie’s request.“I didn’t mind it,” Bre said “She’s hot. Pictured here is Bree Olson with Natalie Kenly, who also reportedly had a threesome with Sheen.

9. Christina Aguilera And Samantha Ronson

Apparently, a while ago Christina Aguilera and Samantha Ronson spent some time together in Mexico for Nicole Richie’s bachelorette party. Really? I mean that doesn't sound like any bad behavior is going to happen at all, right? I think if anyone went to Nicole Richie's bachelorette party they had to know some crazy stuff was going to happen.

The rumor is that Christina and Sam got together and hooked up with someone else, gender unknown, for a little action. Sam’s producer Marlon David confirmed that Sam and Christina did the deed. Apparently he also said Christina was "as horny as a college freshman."

8. Kim Kardashian, Julian St. Jox And Emily Ann

This one is a bit suspect, but who knows? Adult film star Julian St. Jox claims that way back in the day, a decade or so ago, he and his date, another adult star named Emily Ann, had a threesome with Kim Kardashian at a party. Odd that he waited 10 years to tell anyone but he told Star magazine, “she was a little submissive, but she was good. She knew what she was doing. She was very responsive to me … [and] looked like she was enjoying herself very much.” Um, okay.

There have also been rumors that Kim and Kanye West have had threesomes, but they remain just that - rumors.

7. Lady Gaga

Well this one is not a big surprise. Lady Gaga is a bit of a nut, and she also has been open in the past about the fact that she is bisexual. She has admitted that she has previously slept with two people at once, but refused to give any details, including whether she has sex with men or women.

When asked on a Detroit radio talk show if she had a "three-way," she replied: "Umm, well I suppose I have, yes. I don't want to reveal too much. I just can say the truth." She also has said in the past she would be open to a threesome with her fiance Tayler Kinney.

6. Ashton Kutcher And Demi Moore

We already had to guess that these two huge stars might be a tad adventurous, if only because of their huge age difference at the time of their relationship. When they were married, Ashton Kutcher had a mistress name Brittany Jones who claimed that Ashton and Demi Moore had an open relationship.

Not only that, but the two would "share" women in bed. According to Brittany, Demi wasn't that mad about her being a mistress, just that Ashton didn't share her with Demi. Well, I guess if you are as good looking as Ashton and Demi, you might as well spread it around a bit. Right?

5. Jason Biggs And His Wife Jenny Mollen

Jason Biggs is one lucky guy. Jason and Jenny Mollen are married. They not only had a threesome, but she wrote an essay for a blog that was published in Playboy about how hard it was to find a woman for her and Jason to have a threesome with for his birthday.

Turns out she got a hooker, which  “rekindled” their year-long marriage. Yay. I guess. She wrote "so my husband and I got a whore. I’m hoping, unless you’re some sick depraved dissolute of a person, this isn’t the kind of thing you hear everyday.” I guess it is only sick when people that are not famous do it.

4. Olivia Wilde And Jason Sudeikis

This is one of the tamer threesomes on the list, but is a threesome nonetheless. Olivia Wilde and Jason Sudeikis are both very popular actors and one of Hollywood's most cutest couples. On Jason's birthday, she rented out a private room at a strip club for him, complete with two very hot strippers.

According to reports, Jason wasn't allowed to touch the strippers, but Olivia could, and "did." So it seemed like they both got a nice present for Jason's birthday. In fact, maybe Olivia was getting a present for herself as well.

3. Tiger Woods

Good old Tiger. What a guy. He just had to be here right? A threesome? What about a fivesome? I mean come on. However you break it down, the world's most famous sex addict has to be here somewhere.

He was outed by well known madam and escort service owner Michelle Braun, who said that when Tiger ordered women from her, he would often order two, and that he was into girl on girl threesomes. Apparently he was a pretty good golfer, too.

2. Eric Dane And Wife Rebecca Gayheart

This is a fun one. Eric Dane and his wife Rebecca Gayheart were caught on tape fooling around with Keri Ann Peniche. Apparently the tape was stolen and leaked. Both Peniche and Gayheart seemed intoxicated. While Gayheart is married to Dane, she has had some problems in the past including a case of being convicted of vehicular manslaughter.

Peniche is the classic bad girl. The former Miss Teen USA was stripped of her title after posing nude for Playboy and has been on Celebrity Rehab. The tape can be found online. Listening to Gayheart and Peniche talk quickly kills most of the sexiness...fast.

1. Angelina Jolie And Brad Pitt

Say it ain't so. Not Brad Pitt and Angelina Jolie! Well, maybe not, this one is a rumor; there have been a lot of those around these two over the years. These two have been a couple since Brad and Jennifer got divorced (and perhaps even before). Back in 2007, Brad and Angelina were rumored to be having a relationship, one which included a threesome with smoking hot model Karolina Kurkova.

The story has been mentioned numerous times, but no one is totally sure whether it is true or not. For Brad's sake, we sure hope so.
