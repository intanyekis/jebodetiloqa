Diane Sawyer's Net Worth Is Massively Impressive (Even Compared To Her Successor David Muir's)

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Monday, August 5, 2024

Highlights

  • Diane Sawyer's net worth is an impressive $80 million, making her well-set for retirement.
  • Throughout her career, Sawyer has earned numerous accolades and awards for her work in journalism.
  • David Muir, Diane's successor on ABC World News, has a net worth of around $20 million.

Diane Sawyer has had a longstanding career in journalism, fulfilling roles on several different television networks and programs. Sawyer is well known for tackling difficult interviews with high-profile individuals and bringing light to many controversial and complicated topics and issues taking place in the world.

While Diane Sawyer has all but retired from her career in journalism, she has left her mark on the world and continues to educate audiences through her previous interview clips and written word. After dedicating so many years to her craft, what is the status of her net worth, and how does it compare to the news anchor who took her place on ABC World News Tonight?

Diane Sawyer's Current Net Worth Is $80 Million

After decades on television, Diane Sawyer's net worth is impressive

While Diane Sawyer's net worth isn't as high as others in Hollywood journalism, it's nothing to laugh about. Diane's net worth comes in at around 80 million dollars, so it's likely that Sawyer is more than set for her retirement years.

Even after entering retirement, Sawyer's net worth has continued to grow by an additional five million, according to Equity Atlas in 2024.

Throughout the years, Diane Sawyer has won many accolades for her work in journalism. Of these awards, several were Emmys, including one for Excellence in Daytime Programming in 2007, which she tied with the Today Show, and one in 2022 for Outstanding Edited Interview.

Other honors include the GLAAD Media Awards for Excellence in Media 2003 and a Lifetime Achievement Award.

Diane Sawyer Has Earned Her Wealth Through Different Avenues

You might know her from 20/20, but Diane Sawyer has worked on many different shows and various arenas

Throughout her years on television, Diane Sawyer interviewed many top celebrities and public figures. Having gotten her start as a weather forecaster for a small news station, she then went on to work in the press office of the White House for several years.

At this point in her career, Sawyer devoted a great deal of time as a literary aide while helping Richard Nixon write his memoirs, RN: The Memoirs of Richard Nixon, which was released in 1978.

Sawyer went on to be a general news and political correspondent for CBS News and followed that up by being the first female correspondent on 60 Minutes.

After a few years, she moved over to the ABC network. While there, she served as a co-anchor on the show Primetime, which later became 20/20. She later returned to morning television with a stint on Good Morning America.

At the end of 2009, Diane Sawyer took over as the anchor of ABC World News. The journalist served in that role until 2014 when she made the difficult decision to step down around the time of her husband's death.

How Does Diane Sawyer's Net Worth Compare To David Muir's?

David Muir is Diane Sawyer's successor, but he isn't worth as much as she is

When Diane Sawyer announced her departure from ABC World News, the network brought in David Muir as her replacement. He also serves as a co-anchor of the network's show 20/20.

Muir had a similar start to journalism as Sawyer, having gotten his start at a small news station as well. He later went on to join ABC News and worked his way up to the ABC World News sound stage.

Although David Muir works hard to keep certain aspects of his personal life under wraps, it's not as easy to keep your earnings a secret when you are in the entertainment industry. While ABC has not formally released his salary, Hello Magazine speculates that the anchor earns between 5 and 7 million per year for his journalism work on both ABC World News and 20/20. The publication estimates his net worth to be around 20 million.

When comparing Sawyer and Muir's net worth, it's obvious that Diane's sits much higher than David's. However, it's imperative to look at the span of their careers. David Muir was born in 1973, which is when Diane Sawyer was serving in the press office at the White House.

Therefore, she has several more years under her belt in the journalism realm. It's likely that as time progresses, David Muir will find himself at a similar net worth level as his predecessor, the esteemed Diane Sawyer.
