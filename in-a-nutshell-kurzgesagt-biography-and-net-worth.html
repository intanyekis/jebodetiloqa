Kurzgesagt is a German animation studio with a YouTube channel that focuses on minimalist animated educational content, using the flat design style. It discusses scientific, technological, political, philosophical, and psychological subjects.

In A Nutshell Kurzgesagt was founded by Philipp Dettmer in 2013 and currently has 14 million subscriptions and over 1.2 billion views since its establishment.
