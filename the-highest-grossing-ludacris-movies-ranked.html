The Highest-Grossing Ludacris Movies, Ranked

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Monday, June 10, 2024

Quick Links

  • Crash (2004) -$98.4 million
  • No Strings Attached -$149.2 million
  • 2 Fast 2 Furious -$236.4 million
  • Fast & Furious -$363.2 million
  • Fast Five -$626.1 million
  • Fast & Furious Presents:F9  -$721 million
  • Fast & Furious 6 -$788.7 million
  • Furious 7 -$1.516 billion

Ludacris, born Christopher Brian Bridges, is an American rapper, actor, and entrepreneur. He was born on September 11, 1977, in Champaign, Illinois, United States. Ludacris gained prominence in the early 2000s with his debut album, Incognegro, and his breakthrough second album. In addition to his music career, Ludacris has also ventured into acting. He has appeared in several films, including Crash, No Strings Attached, and the Fast & Furious franchise. His acting skills have been recognized with awards and nominations, further establishing his presence in the entertainment industry. Ludacris is known for his philanthropic efforts as well. He founded the Ludacris Foundation, a nonprofit organization that assists urban youth via cultural and educational programs.

Crash (2004) -$98.4 million

Crash is a drama film directed by Paul Haggis and features an ensemble cast. Ludacris played the role of Anthony, and his performance in the film received positive review. The movie explores racial and social tensions in Los Angeles through interconnected storylines. It is vital to note that Ludacris' contribution to the film was substantial in terms of critical praise rather than being a box office draw. The movie grossed $98.4 million worldwide.

No Strings Attached -$149.2 million

No Strings Attached is a romantic comedy film directed by Ivan Reitman and co-produced by Jeffrey Clifford and Joe Medjuck. The film is about two friends, Adam and Emma, who desire to be in a No Strings Attached relationship without falling in love with one other. Ludacris portrays Wallace, a close friend and co-worker of Ashton Kutcher's character, Adam. Ludacris' character provides comedic relief and offers advice to Adam throughout the movie. The film was a success, grossing $149.2 million against a budget of $25 million.

2 Fast 2 Furious -$236.4 million

In 2 Fast 2 Furious (2003), Ludacris takes the role of Tej Parker. Tej is a talented technician and street racer who becomes an essential ally to the film's protagonist, Brian O'Conner (Late Paul Walker). Tej helps Brian and his crew members with their cars and provides technical expertise during high-stakes races and heists. Ludacris' depiction of Tej created the character in the Fast & Furious franchise, and he has repeated the role in subsequent films in the series.

Fast & Furious -$363.2 million

Fast and Furious is an action film directed by Justin Lin and written by Chris Morgan, released in 2009. Ludacris reprises his role as Tej Parker, a skilled mechanic and street racer in the movie. Tej serves as a Dominic Toretto's (Vin Diesel) crew member, providing technical support and expertise during their heists and high-speed pursuits. Ludacris' character, Tej, becomes an integral part of the Fast & Furious series, appearing in multiple subsequent films.

Fast Five -$626.1 million

Fast Five (2011) is an American action film written by Chris Morgan. In the movie Fast Five (2011), Ludacris reprises his role as Tej Parker again. Tej is a skilled mechanic, computer expert, and Dominic Toretto's crew member. In this film, Tej assists in planning and executing a high-stakes heist in Rio de Janeiro alongside Dominic. Ludacris' portrayal of Tej showcases his technical expertise and comedic timing, adding to the dynamic ensemble cast of the Fast & Furious franchise.

Fast & Furious Presents:F9  -$721 million

In the film Fast & Furious Presents: F9 (2021), Ludacris repeats his role as Tej Parker. Tej is a skilled mechanic, hacker, and Dominic Toretto's crew member. In this film, Tej is involved in a new high-stakes adventure as he and the team face off against a dangerous threat. Ludacris brings his charismatic and technical expertise to the role, contributing to the action-packed and thrilling world of the Fast & Furious franchise.

Fast & Furious 6 -$788.7 million

In the film Fast & Furious 6, Ludacris reprise his role as Tej Parker. Tej is a skilled mechanic, hacker, and Dominic Toretto's crew member. In Fast & Furious 6, Tej assists in planning and executing a mission to take down a professional criminal organization led by Owen Shaw. Ludacris' character brings technical expertise, humor, and camaraderie to the team, contributing to the thrilling action and dynamics of the film.

Furious 7 -$1.516 billion

In Furious 7, Ludacris again reprises his role as Tej Parker. Tej now plays the role of a skilled mechanic, hacker, and Dominic Toretto's crew member. In Furious 7, Tej is sent to track down and apprehend a renegade mercenary, Deckard Shaw, who seeks vengeance on the crew for his brother's murder. The team's efforts benefit from the technological know-how and humor of Ludacris' character, which heightens the tension and drama of the movie.
