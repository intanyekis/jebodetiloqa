What Is Rapper Bia Ethnicity? Race Are Her Parents Still Married?

Posted by Jenniffer Sheldon on Wednesday, July 24, 2024

American rapper, Bia is a rapper that belongs to mixed race-ethnicity. Who are her parents? As fans anticipate the new collaboration, we take a look back at Bia’s ethnicity, parents, and more in the following section.

Bianca Landrau, popularly known by her stage name Rapper Bia, is an American rapper.

In addition, Bia is also a composer, model, and vocalist, among other things.

Moreover, she made her TV debut on Oxygen Television’s Sisterhood of Hip Hop.

Bia signed to Pharrell’s ‘I am Other’ record label in collaboration with RCA Records.

Aside from that, Bia is known for a number of songs, such as Safari, Best on Earth, and For Certain.

Bia’s official remix audio for WHOLE LOTTA MONEY, starring Nicki Minaj, was recently released. Her new song is available on YouTube and other music sites.

What Is Rapper Bia Ethnicity And Race?

Rapper Bia belongs to mixed race-ethnicity.

In addition, she belongs mixed race of Italian and Puerto Rican of African descent.

As a result, she identifies as both Latina and Boricua.

Moreover, Bia was born and raised in Medford, Massachusetts, United States. Therefore, she is an American citizen.

Are Rapper Bia Parents Still Married?

Rapper Bia’ opened up about her parents and family while talking to Dazed Digital in the year 2017.

She told that her mother is Italian, while her father is Puerto Rican. We assume that her parents are still married since she has not revealed anything about their separation or divorce.

Bia was raised in a strong family that spoke a mixture of languages.

Bia’s father, according to the VIBE, enjoyed summer cookouts. Her entire family used to get together and listen to music outside.

Bia chooses to keep her personal life private, as do many other celebrities. She hasn’t spoken anything about her parents or their background.

Although we don’t know much about Bia’s parents, we may assume that they are proud of her because she is now a well-known musician throughout the world.

Rapper Bia, also known as Bianca Landrau is at age of 29 years old as of now.
