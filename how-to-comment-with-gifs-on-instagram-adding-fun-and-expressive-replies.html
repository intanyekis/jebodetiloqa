How to Comment with GIFs on Instagram

Posted by Larita Shotwell on Saturday, June 22, 2024

Instagram, the popular photo and video-sharing platform, is constantly evolving to make communication more engaging. One such feature that adds fun and expression to your comments is the ability to use GIFs. In this guide, we’ll walk you through the steps how to comment with GIFs on Instagram.

Step 1: Open the Instagram App

  • Begin by opening the Instagram app on your mobile device. Make sure you’re logged into your Instagram account.

Step 2: Find the Post

  • Navigate to the post on which you want to comment. You can do this by scrolling through your feed or visiting the profile of the user who made the post.

Step 3: Tap on the Comment Section

  • Beneath the post, you’ll find a section where users leave comments. Tap on the “Add a comment…” field to begin typing your comment.

Step 4: Access the GIF Library

  • To add a GIF to your comment, tap on the GIF icon located next to the comment field. It looks like a small GIF sticker.

Step 5: Search for a GIF

  • Instagram will open a library of GIFs that you can search through. You can type keywords related to the type of GIF you want to use. For example, you can type “happy,” “celebrate,” or any other term to find relevant GIFs.

Step 6: Select a GIF

  • Browse through the GIF options and select the one you want to use in your comment by tapping on it.

Step 7: Post Your Comment

  • After selecting a GIF, it will be added to your comment. You can also type any additional text you’d like to include with the GIF. Once you’re satisfied with your comment, tap the “Post” button to submit it.

Step 8: View Your Comment

  • Your comment, now including the GIF, will be visible below the post. Others can view and interact with it just like any other comment.

Commenting with GIFs on Instagram adds a fun and expressive element to your interactions. Whether you’re reacting to a friend’s post, celebrating an achievement, or simply trying to make your comments stand out, using GIFs is a creative way to engage with content.

As Instagram’s features and interface can change over time, it’s a good practice to stay updated with any new developments related to GIF usage or other interactive elements on the platform. Enjoy using GIFs to enhance your Instagram conversations and express yourself in unique ways.
